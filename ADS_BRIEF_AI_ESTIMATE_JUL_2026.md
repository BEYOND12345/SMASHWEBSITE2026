## One-sentence pitch

Smash helps self-employed service workers **send a priced estimate (or quote) in under 60 seconds** — by talking on iPhone or from Gmail — then turn it into an invoice when the customer says yes. No retyping. Their prices, not AI guesswork.

---

## What customers actually struggle with

They do not wake up wanting “invoicing software.”

They struggle with:

1. **Estimating / quoting takes too long** — typing after the site visit, emailing tonight, losing the job to whoever replied first.
2. **Then invoicing is a second admin session** — rebuilding the same job again to get paid.
3. **Generic AI (ChatGPT) drafts wording but invents or forgets their rates.**

So the problem we answer in ads is:

> “I need to send a real price before I lose this job.”

Invoice is how they get paid. **Estimate/quote is how they win the job.** Lead with that.

## What NOT to bid on (yet)

| Avoid / be careful | Why |
|---|---|
| bare “invoice app” / “best invoicing software” | Expensive; Xero / QuickBooks / Fergus territory; weak Smash differentiation |
| “estimating software” / “construction estimating” | Takeoff / BOQ tools (wrong product) |
| bare “instant quote” / “fast quote” | Often insurance / car quote pollution |
| bare “AI quote” without modifiers | Can mix with random/inspirational quote generators — use negatives |
| Competing only on “free invoice generator” | Crowded utility traffic; weaker “win the job” story |

**Negative keyword ideas to seed:** insurance, car insurance, life insurance, inspirational, random quote, gamsat, takeoff, blueprint, bill of quantities, construction takeoff.

## Messaging for ads (copy angles)

**Headline territory (problem → speed → proof):**

- Send the estimate before you lose the job  
- AI estimate from *your* rates — under 60 seconds  
- Talk the job. Send the price.  
- Estimate on site. Invoice when they say yes  

**Proof / differentiators:**

- Voice on iPhone or from Gmail  
- Your catalog prices — not guessed  
- Free to start (5 docs/month)  
- Then one-tap invoice  

**CTA:** Start Free  

**Avoid in customer-facing ads:** “easy,” GPT, Deepgram, machine learning. Show the workflow instead.

## How Ads and organic work together

- **Organic / SEO** is planting long-term ownership of `AI estimate` + voice-to-estimate/invoice.  
- **Ads** should buy the same problem *now* while rankings catch up.  
- Point brand/partner backlinks and ad sitelinks at `/ai-estimates` and `/estimate-generator` first.  
- Success metric for both: **Start Free → first estimate sent → paid** — not clicks alone.
