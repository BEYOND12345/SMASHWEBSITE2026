## Frequently asked questions

**Can I use Google Docs for invoicing in Australia legally?**
Yes. There is no requirement to use specific software for invoicing. A Google Docs invoice is legally valid provided it contains all ATO-required fields, business name, ABN, date, description, total, and GST if applicable. The issue isn't legality, it's accuracy and consistency.

**Is a free invoice template good enough for a sole trader?**
At low invoice volume (1–5 per month) a free template is manageable. At 10+ invoices per month, the accumulated time cost, error rate, and materials capture gap makes a paid invoicing app cost-effective. The break-even point is typically within the first 1–2 months of use.

**Do Google Docs invoice templates include GST calculation?**
Not automatically. You must enter amounts and manually calculate GST, or use a Google Sheets formula. Errors in GST calculation affect your BAS lodgement and can create ATO compliance issues.
