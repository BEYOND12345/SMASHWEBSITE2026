## 3. YouTube SEO Strategy (Quick Wins)

Since you just launched the YouTube channel, here is how to make those videos work for your website SEO:

1. **The "Above the Fold" Link:** The very first line of EVERY YouTube video description must be a link back to the site. 
   *Example:* `Get the free Chrome extension: https://smashinvoices.com/chrome-extension`
2. **Video Chapters:** Add timestamps in the description (e.g., `0:00 Intro`, `0:15 Voice Quoting in Gmail`). Google Search indexes these and shows them as rich snippets.
3. **Embed on the Blog:** For every new video you make, embed it into a relevant blog post on the website. This increases "Time on Page" (a massive Google ranking factor) and gives the YouTube video an external backlink.
4. **Link to YouTube from the Site:** I have already added the YouTube icon to the website footer. *Note: It currently links to `https://youtube.com/@smashinvoices`. If your handle is different, let me know and I will update it!*
