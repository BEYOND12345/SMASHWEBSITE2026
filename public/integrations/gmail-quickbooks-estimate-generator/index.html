Frequently asked questions

Can I import estimates into QuickBooks Online from Excel or CSV?

No — QuickBooks Online blocks native estimate imports across all plans. SMASH bypasses that by reading the customer email or your voice input, matching your catalog, and creating the estimate via API.

Does SMASH replace QuickBooks Online?

No. QuickBooks stays your ledger. SMASH is the Gmail sidebar that builds and sends estimates faster, then syncs the finished record into QBO.

Will my custom line descriptions get overwritten?

Not with Line Lock enabled. You can change the underlying SKU while keeping customer-specific notes frozen in the sidebar.

Which QuickBooks plans work?

Any QuickBooks Online plan that includes estimates. Connect once with OAuth and pick your company file.

Is customer data stored on your servers?

SMASH uses OAuth and only processes the email thread you have open. We do not sell financial records or customer lists.
