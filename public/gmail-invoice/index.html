What is the best Gmail invoice extension?

SMASH is a Chrome extension that adds an invoice and quote builder to Gmail. It reads open customer emails (From Email), accepts voice dictation (Start Recording), applies your saved pricing, generates GST- or VAT-ready PDFs, and lets you send from the same thread with approval and pay links. Completed documents sync to Xero or QuickBooks Online.

Frequently asked questions

Does SMASH work inside Gmail on mobile?

The Gmail invoice extension runs in Chrome on desktop and laptop. For mobile, use the SMASH iOS app for voice invoicing on site.

Can I send invoices without Xero or QuickBooks?

Yes. SMASH generates and sends standalone PDF invoices and quotes. Accounting sync is optional on paid plans.

How is this different from Gmail add-ons like templates?

Templates still need you to type prices and line items. SMASH reads the email or your voice and builds the full document with tax and totals.

Is it free to try?

Yes — five invoices or quotes per month at no cost. No credit card required to install.

What countries are supported?

Australia, New Zealand, United Kingdom, United States, and Canada — with local tax handling.

How do I install it?

Add SMASH from the Chrome Web Store, sign in, connect Gmail permissions, and open any customer email to see the sidebar.
