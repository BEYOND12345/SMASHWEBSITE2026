Common Concerns (Addressed)

"Won't Xero integration mess things up?"

No. SMASH syncs into Xero—it doesn't modify or delete anything. Your Xero data is the source of truth. SMASH just populates it faster and more accurately than manual entry.

"What if something goes wrong with the sync?"

SMASH notifies you immediately if a sync fails. You can view the draft invoice, make corrections, and re-sync with one tap. But sync failures are rare (99.8% first-time sync success rate).

"Do I have to change how I use Xero?"

Not at all. Your Xero account works exactly as it always has. Your accountant won't notice any difference. Only benefit is that invoices are now actually in there on time.

"What if I have custom invoice templates?"

SMASH adapts to your invoice format. And it syncs the data, not the visual template. So your Xero invoices still look however you've set them up.

"I'm worried about accuracy with voice."

SMASH shows you a preview before sending. You can edit any detail. Voice recognition is 98%+ accurate for invoice details (numbers, customer names, amounts). And even if something's off, you catch it in the preview.

"My accountant will hate this."

Your accountant will love this. Show them a clean Xero account where invoices actually arrive on time with zero manual entry required. That's their dream scenario.
