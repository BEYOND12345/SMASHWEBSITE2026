Short answer

ChatGPT can draft a quote. It cannot run your quoting business.

It will write professional-looking estimates if you paste labour, materials, and GST instructions every time. It will not hold your real rates, send an instant quote while the customer is still deciding, or convert that quote into a payable tax invoice when the job is done.

If you need the product: AI estimates · Voice quote / invoice on iPhone · Free estimate generator.

What people actually paste into ChatGPT

Typical prompt:

“Write a quote for replacing a kitchen tap for Sarah Jones. Two hours labour, compression fittings, call-out fee, GST for Australia.”

That prompt is the whole product use case. The person already knows the job. They want a priced document, not a creative writing exercise.

ChatGPT helps with wording. The pain is everything after:

  1. Did it use your hourly rate?
  2. Did it remember your call-out fee?
  3. Can you send it in 30 seconds from the driveway?
  4. Can you turn it into an invoice without retyping?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can ChatGPT write a quote for a tradie?

It can draft wording and rough line items if you type every rate in. It cannot remember your catalog, apply your call-out fees automatically, or send a branded PDF while you are still on site.

Is a ChatGPT estimate good enough to win jobs?

Sometimes as a rough guide. For winning work, customers want a clear priced document fast. The first real quote usually wins — not the best paragraph later tonight.

What should I use instead of ChatGPT for quotes?

A dedicated quoting tool that prices from your catalog. SMASH lets you talk the scope on iPhone, verify lines, and send an instant quote in about 30 seconds, then convert to invoice when the job is done.

Can ChatGPT create invoices and quotes the same way?

Same limitation on both. It drafts text. It does not hold GST rules that stick to your business, sequential numbers, payment links, or Xero/QuickBooks sync.

How is voice quoting different from pasting into ChatGPT?

You speak once against saved prices. Unmatched items are flagged for you to price — not silently guessed. Then you send. ChatGPT makes you re-enter context every session.
