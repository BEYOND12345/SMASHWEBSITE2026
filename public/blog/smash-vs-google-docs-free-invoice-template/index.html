The Sunday night problem

I used Google Docs for two years. Every Sunday night, laptop open, cup of tea going cold, trying to reconstruct the week from memory.

Tuesday's job. What did I charge for labour? What materials did I use? Did I get a good drain snake from Bunnings or did I already have one? I'd guess. I'd round. I'd undercharge by $20 here, forget materials there.

What the Google Docs template actually requires

A Google Docs invoice template needs you to:

  1. Open the document
  2. Duplicate the template for a new invoice
  3. Type the client name and details
  4. Type the date
  5. Add a new invoice number (manually tracked)
  6. Type each service line
  7. Enter prices from memory
  8. Calculate subtotal
  9. Calculate GST
  10. Calculate total
  11. Save as PDF
  12. Email or WhatsApp it

Frequently asked questions

Can I use Google Docs for invoicing in Australia legally? Yes. There is no requirement to use specific software for invoicing. A Google Docs invoice is legally valid provided it contains all ATO-required fields, business name, ABN, date, description, total, and GST if applicable. The issue isn't legality, it's accuracy and consistency.

Is a free invoice template good enough for a sole trader? At low invoice volume (1–5 per month) a free template is manageable. At 10+ invoices per month, the accumulated time cost, error rate, and materials capture gap makes a paid invoicing app cost-effective. The break-even point is typically within the first 1–2 months of use.

Do Google Docs invoice templates include GST calculation? Not automatically. You must enter amounts and manually calculate GST, or use a Google Sheets formula. Errors in GST calculation affect your BAS lodgement and can create ATO compliance issues.

What's the simplest invoicing upgrade from Google Docs? SMASH Invoices is the most direct upgrade for on-site workers, you replace the typing with speaking and get the same professional output in a fraction of the time. The first invoice takes about 15 minutes to set up (upload one old Google Docs invoice to teach it your rates), then every subsequent invoice is under 60 seconds.

Can SMASH Invoices import my existing Google Docs invoice template? Yes. During setup, upload one of your existing invoices, including one created in Google Docs, as a PDF. SMASH reads the line items, rates, and descriptions and builds your personal pricing catalogue from them. Your first voice invoice uses your existing prices automatically.
