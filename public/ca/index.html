FAQ

Does SMASH handle GST, HST and PST in Canada?

Yes. Pick your province during onboarding — Ontario (HST 13%), BC (GST + PST), Alberta (GST only), Quebec (GST + QST), and all others — and SMASH applies the correct tax combination to every invoice automatically. No manual maths.

Can I use SMASH as a Canadian sole proprietor or small business?

Yes. SMASH is built for Canadian sole proprietors, independent contractors, and small service businesses. Your Business Number (BN) appears on every invoice and the correct provincial tax is applied automatically.

Is SMASH available in Canada?

Yes — SMASH is live on the Canadian App Store and as a Chrome extension. Free to start, no credit card required.

Will pricing be in CAD?

Invoices you send your customers default to CAD. Subscription pricing is billed in AUD on the same plan everywhere — same flat rate for every country, no per-user fees.

Will there be French language support for Quebec?

English at launch. French UI and French-language invoices for Quebec contractors are on the roadmap. If French-language invoicing is important to you, vote for it on the roadmap page.
