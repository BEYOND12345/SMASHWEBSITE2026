Why external mail relays keep failing

Web apps like QuickBooks often send through external mail relays. Modern Gmail security, SPF alignment, and DMARC checks flag those messages — so invoices bounce, land in spam, or never leave the queue. In the Canada, that means delayed cash flow and awkward follow-ups with clients who never received your bill.

Send from your active Gmail session
