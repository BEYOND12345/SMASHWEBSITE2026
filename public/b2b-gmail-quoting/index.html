FAQ

Do I need Xero or QuickBooks to use SMASH?

No. SMASH works as a fully featured, standalone quoting and invoicing tool. It manages your items, tracks clients, creates PDFs, and processes payments entirely on its own. Xero and QuickBooks sync is an optional power-tool addition available when you want it — not required to get started.

How does the PDF and email parsing work?

SMASH reads the email text or attached PDF natively inside your secure sidebar sandbox. It extracts the client’s order profile, identifies the raw line items, and instantly maps them to your internal catalog codes. The perfect draft document is ready to validate in 10 seconds flat.

Can I use this to completely replace Wave or legacy billing software?

Yes. If you are looking for a streamlined, lightweight alternative to escape platform changes, SMASH operates as an independent standalone system, giving you matching billing and Stripe capabilities directly inside Gmail.

Are my clients forced to create an account to pay me?

Not at all. They receive a standard web link accessible on any device where they can sign off on quotes or complete payments via Stripe in seconds.

Is my financial and email data secure?

Absolutely. Your local parameters, catalog files, and tokens are safely isolated within your browser storage sandbox. Xero and QuickBooks connect exclusively via official, bank-grade OAuth 2.0 pipelines. Voice inputs are instantly converted to plain text locally — no audio is ever logged, stored, or shared. Financial data is never shared with third parties.
