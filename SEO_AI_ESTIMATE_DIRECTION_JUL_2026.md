## North star (unchanged)

> The fastest way for self-employed service workers to invoice — by voice.

## 90-day growth door (new)

> **Own the problem: “I need to send a priced estimate before I lose the job.”**  
> **Primary search term: `AI estimate`**  
> **Mechanism: voice (and Gmail) → your catalog prices → send in under 60 seconds → one-tap invoice when they say yes.**

Voice to invoice is **not** dropped. It is the moat and the close. AI estimate is the **door people search for**.

```
Search: AI estimate / job estimate / estimate generator
    → /ai-estimates or /estimate-generator
    → Start Free
    → Send priced estimate
    → Quote/estimate → invoice
    → Paid plan
```

## Messaging rules

1. **Problem first:** send the estimate before you lose the job.  
2. **Mechanism second:** voice on iPhone / Gmail at the desk — your prices, not guessed.  
3. **Close third:** one-tap invoice → get paid (keeps Smash Invoices brand honest).  
4. **Speed proof:** under 60 seconds / before you leave the driveway — never vague “easy.”  
5. **CTA:** Start Free (iOS + Chrome where relevant).  
6. **Do not** change `/voice-invoicing` title or meta without explicit instruction.

### Language by market (same URLs)

- Global / US / CA default copy: **estimate**  
- AU / NZ / UK body + FAQ: also **quote**  
- Never build separate thin country clones for the same intent unless hreflang requires it
